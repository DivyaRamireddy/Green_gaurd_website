Powdery Mildew

White powder-like spots on leaves; caused by fungal infection. Common in warm, dry conditions.

Bacterial Blight

Dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems; spreads rapidly in humid weather.

Root Rot

Caused by overwatering and poor soil drainage; roots become brown and mushy.

Leaf Spot Disease

Small brown or black circular spots on leaves, leading to yellowing and premature drop.

Rust Disease

Orange or reddish pustules appear on leaf undersides; affects cereal and vegetable crops.

Wilt Disease

Caused by fungi that block water flow; plants appear droopy even with adequate moisture.
