Causes & Practical Remedies

Fungal Infections (e.g., Blight, Rust, Mildew)

Causes: High humidity, poor air circulation, infected seed or soil.

Remedies: Remove infected leaves, ensure spacing and sunlight, use biological fungicides (Trichoderma), neem/sulfur sprays as organic control.

Bacterial Diseases (e.g., Bacterial Leaf Blight)

Causes: Contaminated tools, infected transplants, water splashes.

Remedies: Sanitize tools, remove infected plants, avoid overhead irrigation, use copper-based treatments sparingly under expert guidance.

Viral Diseases (e.g., Mosaic, Leaf Curl)

Causes: Spread by insect vectors like aphids/whiteflies and contaminated material.

Remedies: Eradicate infected plants, control vectors with traps/biocontrol, use virus-resistant varieties where available.

Root Rot & Wilt

Causes: Waterlogged soil, poor drainage, soil-borne pathogens (Fusarium, Pythium).

Remedies: Improve drainage, avoid overwatering, solarize soil, use well-composted organic matter, rotate crops.
