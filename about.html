About GreenGuard

GreenGuard is a student-led Community Service Project aimed at educating farmers and communities about plant diseases and sustainable management strategies. The site provides easy-to-understand information on common diseases, their symptoms and eco-friendly remedies. Our goal is to promote healthy crops, reduce unnecessary pesticide use, and support long-term soil and environmental health.

Team

Developed by Divya Reddy and team — CSE (Data Science), Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering and Technology.

How to use

  1. Browse Diseases to learn symptoms and remedies.
  2. Use Symptoms page to match field observations.
  3. Apply recommended sustainable solutions and contact local agri-officers for heavy outbreaks.
